## Business Insights:
- High-value customers should be prioritized through retention and loyalty strategies.
- Medium-value customers offer opportunities for upselling and increased engagement.
- Low-value customers require optimized, low-cost marketing actions.

## Recommendations:
- Focus marketing investment on high-impact customer segments.
- Design personalized campaigns based on behavioral patterns.
- Monitor segment evolution over time to adjust strategies.

## Expected Impact:
- Increased customer lifetime value.
- Improved marketing ROI.
- Better alignment between customer behavior and business actions.
